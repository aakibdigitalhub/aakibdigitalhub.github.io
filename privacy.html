Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Aakib Digital Hub is run by one person, not a company with a data team — so this policy is meant to be plain and honest rather than legal padding. It explains what I collect through this website, why, and what I do (and don't do) with it.

What I collect

The only information this site collects is what you choose to give me through the contact form:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • The service you're interested in
  • Whatever project details you write in the message box

This site does not use cookies, analytics, or advertising trackers of any kind. I don't know who visits this page unless you fill out the form and send it to me.

Why I collect it

Purely to reply to you. When you submit the contact form, those details are used to understand your project and get back to you by email — nothing more.

How it's stored

Contact form submissions are kept only for as long as needed to respond to you and, if we start working together, for the length of the project. I don't sell, rent, or share your details with third parties, and I don't use them for marketing unless you separately ask to hear from me.

Third-party services this site uses

To keep the site fast and good-looking, a few external services are loaded when the page renders:

  • Google Fonts — for the typefaces used on this page.
  • Tailwind CSS (CDN) — for styling.
  • Chart.js (CDN) — to draw the chart on the homepage.

These are code libraries, not tracking scripts, and none of them are configured here to collect personal data. Depending on your location, loading fonts from Google's servers may involve your browser making a request to Google — standard for most modern websites.

Your rights

You can ask me at any time what information I have about you, ask me to correct it, or ask me to delete it. Just email me and I'll sort it out directly — no ticket system, no runaround.

Changes to this policy

If anything about how this site collects or handles data changes, I'll update this page and change the date at the top.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data — email aakibdigitalhub@gmail.com.
